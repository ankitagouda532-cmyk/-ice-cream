Fresh, Fruity & Naturally Sweet

Strawberry ice cream is made using fresh, ripe strawberries that give it a naturally sweet and delicious fruity flavor. The pink color and creamy texture make it one of the most refreshing flavors.

Why People Love It

  • Made from real strawberries
  • Refreshing fruity taste
  • Beautiful natural pink color
  • Perfect for summer

Ingredients

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Milk
  • Fresh cream
  • Sugar
  • Natural strawberry pulp

Fun Facts

  • Strawberry is the third most popular ice cream flavor in the world.
  • The flavor originally became famous in Europe in the 1800s.
  • Fresh strawberries give the ice cream its natural color.

Best Pairings

Strawberry ice cream goes great with:

  • Chocolate syrup
  • Vanilla cake
  • Waffles
  • Strawberry pieces

Serving Tip

Top it with fresh strawberry slices for extra flavor and beauty.

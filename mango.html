Tropical, Sweet & Made with Real Mango

Mango ice cream is one of the most loved flavors, especially in India. Made using ripe Alphonso or Kesar mangoes, this ice cream delivers a rich, tropical taste that feels like summer in every bite.

Why People Love It

  • Made from fresh, juicy mango pulp
  • Natural golden-yellow color
  • Rich, tropical, fruity flavor
  • Perfect dessert for hot weather

Ingredients

  • Fresh mango pulp
  • Milk
  • Fresh cream
  • Sugar
  • A pinch of cardamom (optional)

Fun Facts

  • Mango is known as the “King of Fruits.”
  • Alphonso mango adds the best flavor to ice cream.
  • Mango ice cream is one of India's top 5 most sold flavors.

Best Pairings

Mango ice cream goes well with:

  • Waffles
  • Mango pieces
  • Dry fruits
  • Vanilla cake

Serving Tip

Serve chilled with extra mango chunks for a richer fruity experience.

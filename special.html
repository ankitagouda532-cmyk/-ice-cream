Unique, Creative & Delicious Flavors

These special flavors bring a twist to the classic ice creams. Each one is crafted with unique ingredients to give you a new and exciting taste experience.

Our Special Flavors

  • Butterscotch – Sweet, buttery, and topped with crunchy praline.
  • Pista (Pistachio) – Soft, nutty flavor made with real pista.
  • Black Currant – Tangy and sweet berry flavor with purple color.
  • Oreo Crunch – Vanilla ice cream loaded with Oreo cookie pieces.

Why These Flavors Are Special

  • Unique taste not available everywhere
  • Perfect for adventurous ice cream lovers
  • Made with premium and fresh ingredients
  • Beautiful colors and textures

Best Pairings

These special flavors go well with:

  • Chocolate syrup
  • Waffle cones
  • Dry fruits
  • Caramel sauce

Serving Tip

Serve in a waffle cup for extra crunch and flavor.
